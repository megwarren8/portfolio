Privacy

Nothing here tracks you.

No analytics, no ads, no tracking scripts, no social pixels, on this site or any other megan-warren.com property. Nothing about your visit is collected or profiled, and nothing is ever sold or shared with advertisers. That is the whole policy. The rest of this page just spells it out.

The details, plainly

Contact and coaching forms
What you type is sent to me as an email, and that is it. A small Cloudflare Worker relays the message, and a hidden honeypot field filters spam bots, so there is no CAPTCHA and no third-party form service. Your message is never stored in any database.
The course textbooks
The probability, trigonometry, and derivatives books save your reading preferences (font size, dark mode, the read-aloud voice) in your own browser's localStorage. That data never leaves your device. A service worker caches the book on your device so repeat visits load fast; that cache is local-only too.
Ask the TA
The chat inside the textbooks is the one place anything is kept: conversations are saved so a student's teacher can review them. Students enter a class passphrase, not a name or an account, and should not put personal information in the chat. Messages are processed by Anthropic's Claude API to generate replies.
Teacher pages
Teacher hubs and settings pages sit behind a login. The settings pages set one session cookie, and its only job is keeping the teacher signed in.

The rest of it

Hosting
Every site is served by Cloudflare, which may set strictly necessary security cookies to serve the site safely.
Daily Lesson Kit
lessons.megan-warren.com stores all lesson data in the teacher's own browser localStorage. The optional Google Drive backup runs only when the signed-in teacher clicks it.
Why there is no cookie banner
Cookie banners exist to ask your consent for tracking. These sites do not track, so there is nothing to ask. The missing banner is not an oversight; it is the point.
Questions
Use the contact form on the home page. I read every message myself.
