1:1 AI Coaching

Prefer to learn to do it yourself?

Watching someone else use a tool does not teach most people something they can repeat tomorrow. I start by learning how you already work and build a plan around it. Then I sit next to you while you drive, in person if you're within five miles of downtown Boston, over Zoom everywhere else. You leave able to do it again without me.

How it works

1. First conversation
A real conversation, fifteen to twenty minutes, free. Where you are now, what you've already tried, what stuck, what didn't, and what you want to be able to do on your own.
2. Your game plan
A short, written plan built around that conversation, a lesson plan, not a template. You see exactly what we're covering and why before you spend a dollar.
3. Guided practice
Forty-five to sixty minutes, hands-on. You drive, I scaffold: enough support that you never get stuck, never so much that I do it for you.
